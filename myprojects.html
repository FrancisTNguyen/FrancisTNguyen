Battleground Mars

This is a multiplayer shooter game that I worked on at Refactor Games! As a engineering and level design intern, I worked on many features such as programming the muzzle flash particle system and designing our Conquest level. While I wasn't the one that created the initial design for the map, I've made many improvements to it such as creating better visual language by highlighting points of interest with the landscape painting tool! I've also included an extra layer to the level by introducing a tunnel that players can take to get from one point to another. Finally, one of the last things that I did for this project was to update our landscape with meshes that would improve immersion.
