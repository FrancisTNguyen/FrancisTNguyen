Action Hero (In Progress!)

I'm currently the game designer and programmer for an endless runner game called "Action Hero". I found myself with small pockets of time during the day where I get bored, but just not enough time to play a full video game on my PC. So I wanted to create a game that I can play during these short down times. I was inspired by endless runner games like Temple Run and Google's Dino Run. None of them required a time commitment to play, but its fast paced gameplay kept players hooked long enough for whatever they were waiting for. In fact, Dino Run was created for users that were waiting for their internet to come back up. I want to provide that same fast pace experience, but with some elements of a third person shooter.
