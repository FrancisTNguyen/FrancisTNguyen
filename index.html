Hello, my name is Francis! I am a game designer and a gameplay programmer. My goal as a designer is to create fun and compelling video games. I love to take inspiration from video games that I've played and put new and fun twists to them! As a designer, I am always finding and learning new methods to implement within my projects. I believe that my engineering background has helped me become a better problem solver, whether I am debugging an engineering feature or tackling a design problem. Most of my projects has been in Unreal Engine 4, C++, or Python, but I am constantly learning new tech as well. Feel free to look around and don't hesitate to contact me!
